What Makes PALM Different?

The PALM technique transforms meetings from discussion forums into decision engines by focusing on four critical elements:

Traditional Meetings
  • Focus on topics to discuss
  • Invite everyone who might be interested
  • Default to 30 or 60 minutes
  • Create agendas of discussion points
  • End with "next steps" and follow-ups
PALM Meetings
  • Focus on decisions to make
  • Invite only those needed for decisions
  • Set the minimum viable time limit
  • Create agendas of decisions to make
  • End with clear decisions documented
